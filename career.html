WORK CULTURE


Work culture plays an important role in extracting the best out of employees and making them stick to the organization for a longer duration. At ROY ELECTRICALS we staunchly believe that a healthy work culture leads to satisfied employees and an increased productivity. We strive to maintain the open culture often associated with start-ups, in which everyone is a hands-on contributor and feels comfortable sharing ideas and opinions.

As a result of strong infusion of talent across industry verticals to create a strong system-driven work processes, Roy Electricals is smoothly able to adapt to changing market trends and business dynamics. Growth of an Employee is monitored & governed by "KPA – Key Performance Areas" which are prepared by consensus. A mechanism for assesing the performance which also provides an assessment for incorporating training programs to enhance skills of employees.

The Friendlier and focused team at Roy Electricals is receptive to new views and ideas to improve the quality of life at work, and create employee friendly policies and practical guidelines.
